2021 - 2023

illustrator

Adobe Illustrator is one of the programs specialized in vector graphic design. Illustrator is one of the essential design tools used by graphic designers and commercial artists to create accurate and creative vector graphics. Illustrator provides a wide range of tools and features that allow users to create logos, graphics, motion graphics, etc. with high precision and premium quality. The program features the ability to edit text, manipulate colors, apply shadows and shading, and control shape templates. If you're looking for an advanced and versatile design tool, Adobe Illustrator is the perfect choice.

2022 - 2023

cnava

If you're looking for a design tool that's easy to use and accessible to everyone, Canva is the perfect choice for you. Canva is an online design software that allows users to create professional graphic designs easily and without the need for advanced design skills. Canva provides a wide range of ready-made templates, graphics, images, and fonts that you can use to create different designs such as greeting cards, posters, presentations, social media, and more. You can customize designs and add personal text and images with ease. Canva is a great tool for individuals, small businesses, and marketers who need attractive designs quickly and easily.
